About Us



Company Profile

SBNGD Security is a premier security services provider headquartered in Randburg, South Africa. Established in 2020, we deliver tailored solutions to ensure safety and peace of mind across various sectors, including residential estates, public institutions, commercial properties, and events.

Our team is composed of highly trained professionals equipped with the latest technology to respond effectively to threats and incidents. With a strong focus on integrity, professionalism, and accountability, we have earned the trust of numerous clients and partners across the country.

In response to the growing crime rates, our mission is to be a reliable and efficient force that supports law enforcement by protecting people, properties, and public spaces.

Our Services

Manned Guarding

Our well-trained security officers provide 24/7 physical presence at commercial, industrial, and residential sites. They deter criminal activity and act quickly in emergencies.

Mobile Patrols

Our patrol vehicles conduct regular and randomized patrols with high visibility, equipped with GPS and two-way radios for rapid response and deterrence.

Alarm Monitoring & Response

State-of-the-art alarm systems monitored 24/7 by our control room. Our response teams are dispatched instantly to assess and neutralize threats.

Access Control

We implement and maintain biometric, card-based, and keypad entry systems to manage who enters your premises—ideal for businesses and high-security zones.

Event Security

From concerts and sports events to private gatherings, our experienced teams provide crowd control, VIP protection, and emergency response management.
