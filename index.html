Who We Are

Malilani Commodities UK Ltd is a London-based strategic commodities sourcing and trading firm, specializing in high-value mineral exports from sub-Saharan Africa. We connect vetted African producers of copper cathodes and gold with global buyers through secure, transparent, and compliant trading structures.

Our Services

  • Copper Cathode & Gold Trading – Verified DRC-origin materials with SGS/BV inspection.
  • CIF & FOB Trade Structuring – Escrow, LC, and UCP600-compliant mechanisms.
  • Mandate Representation – Legal, vetted representation of licensed exporters.

Why Choose Us

We are a UK-registered entity with reliable legal, banking, and compliance infrastructure. Our team combines international trade expertise with deep African supply chain access.
