About Us

SBNGD Security is a premier security services provider headquartered in Randburg, South Africa. Established in 2020, we deliver tailored solutions to ensure safety and peace of mind across various sectors, including residential estates, public institutions, commercial properties, and events.

Our team is composed of highly trained professionals equipped with the latest technology to respond effectively to threats and incidents. With a strong focus on integrity, professionalism, and accountability, we have earned the trust of numerous clients and partners across the country.

In response to the growing crime rates, our mission is to be a reliable and efficient force that supports law enforcement by protecting people, properties, and public spaces.
